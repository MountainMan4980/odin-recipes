Jalapeno Poppers

Description

Delicious cheese filled jalapenos! This recipe will guide you to cook a great treat for parties and family get-togethers. These Poppers are vegetarian and are best made with freshest ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Jalapenos
  • Cream Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Chives
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Cut jalapenos in half
  2. Mix a small amount of Cheddar cheese into Cream Cheese in a mixing bowl
  3. Add a dash of Chives and Garlic powder
  4. Add cheese mixture to each popper
  5. Bake at 350 degrees F for 20 minutes
