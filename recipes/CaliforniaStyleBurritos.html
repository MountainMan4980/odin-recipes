California Burrito

Description

Another Southwestern classic! Souther California's take on a iconic mexican food entree. Make sure you add the Aoili!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Diced Sirloin
  • French Fries
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Guacamole
  • Mixed Spices
  • Chipotle Aoili
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Cook Diced Sirloin on stovetop unitl it is brown all the way through
  2. Prepare French Fries in Deep Fryer
  3. Add Sirloin to tortilla
  4. Add French Fries on top of Sirloin
  5. Quickly add Cheddar cheese
  6. Add Guacamole
  7. Generously add Chipotle Aoili
  8. Top it off with a pinch of Cilantro
