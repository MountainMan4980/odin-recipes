Green Chile Cheese Burgers

Description

A New Mexican classic! Spice up (literally) your cheese burger with green chile and other southwest inspired toppings

Ingredients

  • 93/7% lean ground beef
  • American Cheese
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Roasted Green Chile
  • Guacamole

Steps

  1. Preheat Grill to 350 degrees F
  2. Make four circular patties out of 1lb of 93/7 lean ground beef
  3. Grill Patties unitl internal temperature reaches 150 degrees F, flipping every 3-5 minutes
  4. Add one slice of american cheese to each patty
  5. Add green chile on top of the cheese
  6. Let the cheeseburger stay on the grill for 2 minutes to melt cheese
  7. Remove Burger from Grill and add Guacamole
  8. add your favorite toppings and enjoy!
